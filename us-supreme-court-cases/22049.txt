United States Supreme Court ORDER LIST 07/17/07, (2007) No. 071707 Argued:     Decided: July 17, 2007
(ORDER LIST: 551 U.S.)
TUESDAY, JULY 17, 2007
ORDER
IT IS ORDERED that the revised Rules of this Court, today approved by the Court and lodged with the Clerk, shall be effective October 1, 2007, and be printed as an appendix to the United States Reports.
IT IS FURTHER ORDERED that the Rules promulgated May 2, 2005, see 544 U.S. 1071, shall be rescinded as of September 30, 2007, and that the revised Rules shall govern all proceedings in cases commenced after that date and, to the extent feasible and just, cases then pending.
