10-868 CATE, SEC., CA DOC, ET AL. V. PIRTLE, JOHN H.
The motions of respondents John H. Pirtle, Robert Johnson, Anthony Sneed, and Michael C. Slater for leave to proceed in forma pauperis are granted. The petition for a writ of certiorari as to John H. Pirtle, Robert Johnson, Anthony Sneed, and Michael C. Slater is granted. The judgments are
vacated, and the cases are remanded to the United States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit for further consideration in light of Swarthout v. Cooke, 562 U.S. ___ (2011) (per curiam).
The petition for a writ of certiorari as to Ron Mosley is
denied.
10-987 AFFILIATED COMPUTER SERVICES V. FENSTERSTOCK, JOSHUA G.
The petition for a writ of certiorari is granted. The
judgment is vacated, and the case is remanded to the United
States Court of Appeals for the Second Circuit for further
consideration in light of AT&T Mobility LLC v. Concepcion,
563 U.S. ___ (2011).
10-1070 EISAI CO., LTD., ET AL. V. TEVA PHARMACEUTICALS USA, INC.
The petition for a writ of certiorari is granted. The
judgment is vacated, and the case is remanded to the United
States Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit with
instructions to dismiss the case as moot. See United States
v. Munsingwear, Inc., 340 U.S. 36 (1950).
