United States Supreme Court ORDER LIST 07/18/14, (2014) No. 071814zr Argued:     Decided: October 9, 2014
(ORDER LIST: 573 U.S.)
FRIDAY, JULY 18, 2014 ORDER IN PENDING CASE 14A65      HERBERT, GOV. OF UT, ET AL. V. EVANS, JONELL, ET AL.
The application for stay presented to Justice Sotomayor and by her referred to the Court is granted. The preliminary injunction issued by the United States District Court for the District of Utah, case No. 2:14-cv-00055-DAK, on May 19, 2014, is stayed pending final disposition of the appeal by the United States Court of Appeals for the Tenth Circuit.
