The motion of respondent for leave to proceed in forma
pauperis is granted. The petition for a writ of certiorari
is granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded
to the United States Court of Appeals for the Sixth Circuit
for further consideration in light of Carey v. Musladin,
549 U. S. ___ (2006), and Schriro v. Landrigan, 550 U.S. ___
(2007). Justice Stevens, Justice Ginsburg, and Justice Breyer
would deny the petition for writ of certiorari.
