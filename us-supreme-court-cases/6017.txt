United States Supreme Court DOUGHTY v. MAXWELL, (1963) No. 516 Argued:     Decided: April 22, 1963
Certiorari granted; judgment vacated; and case remanded.
Reported below: 173 Ohio St. 407, 183 N. E. 2d 368.
Petitioner pro se.
Mark McElroy, Attorney General of Ohio, and James E. Rattan, Assistant Attorney General, for respondent.
PER CURIAM.
The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petition for writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded for further consideration in light of Gideon v. Wainwright, 372 U.S. 335 . [372 U.S. 781, 782]
