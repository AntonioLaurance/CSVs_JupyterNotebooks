United States Supreme Court UNIVERSAL TRADES v. PENNSYLVANIA, (1958) No. 434 Argued:     Decided: December 8, 1958
Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.
Reported below: 392 Pa. 323, 141 A. 2d 204.
Manuel Kraus and Roy J. Keefer for appellant.
Thomas D. McBride, Attorney General of Pennsylvania, and Edward Friedman, Deputy Attorney General, for appellee.
PER CURIAM.
The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.
MR. JUSTICE FRANKFURTER took no part in the consideration or decision of this case. [358 U.S. 129, 130]
