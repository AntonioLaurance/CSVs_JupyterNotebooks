United States Supreme Court ORDER LIST 06/01/10, (2010) No. 060110 Argued:     Decided: June 7, 2010
(ORDER LIST: 560 U.S.)
TUESDAY, JUNE 1, 2010
ORDER IN PENDING CASE
09A1133     MCCOMISH, JOHN, ET AL. V. BENNETT, AZ SEC. OF STATE, ET AL.
The application to vacate the stay presented to Justice Kennedy and by him referred to the Court is denied without prejudice to a renewed application if the parties represent that they intend to file a timely petition for a writ of certiorari before this Court. The motion of Buzz Mills for Governor Campaign for leave to file a brief as amicus curiae is granted.
