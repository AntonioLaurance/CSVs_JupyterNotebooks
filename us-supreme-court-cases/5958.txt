United States Supreme Court HELVERING v. PAN-AMERICAN LIFE INS CO, (1940) No. 264 Argued: November 19, 1940    Decided: December 9, 1940
Mr. Robert H. Jackson, Atty. Gen., and Arnold Raum, of Washington, D. C., for petitioner. [311 U.S. 272, 273]   Mr. Eugene J. McGivney, of New Orleans, La., for respondent.
Mr. Justice BLACK delivered the opinion of the Court.
This case involves respondent's income tax for the tax year 1933. It is in all respects governed by our decision in Helvering v. Oregon Mutual Life Ins. Co., 311 U.S. 267 , 61 S.Ct. 207, this day decided, and on the authority of that case the decision below is
Affirmed.
