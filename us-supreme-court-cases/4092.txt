CERTIORARI -- SUMMARY DISPOSITIONS
06-1454 ) SEMPRA GENERATION, ET AL. V. PUBLIC UTILITIES COMM., ET AL.
)
06-1468 ) DYNERGY POWER MARKETING, INC. V. PUBLIC UTILITIES COMM., ET AL.
The petitions for writs of certiorari are granted. The
judgment is vacated and the cases are remanded to the United
States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit for further
consideration in light of Morgan Stanley Capital Group Inc. v.
Public Util. Dist. No. 1 of Snohomish Cty., 554 U.S. ___ (2008).
The Chief Justice took no part in the consideration or decision
of these petitions.
07-8018 YOUNGER, DARRELL V. CALIFORNIA
The motion of petitioner for leave to proceed in forma
pauperis and the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted.
The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the Court of
Appeal of California, First Appellate District, for further
consideration in light of Giles v. California, 554 U.S. ___
(2008).
