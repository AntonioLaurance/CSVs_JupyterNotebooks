United States Supreme Court ORDER LIST 05/12/16, (2016) No. 051216zr Argued:     Decided: May 13, 2016
(ORDER LIST: 578 U.S.)
THURSDAY, MAY 12, 2016 ORDER IN PENDING CASE 15A1175     DUNN, COMM'R, AL DOC V. Madison, Vernon
The application to vacate the stay of execution of sentence of death entered by the United States Court of Appeals for the Eleventh Circuit on May 12, 2016, presented to Justice Thomas and by him referred to the Court, is denied.
The Chief Justice, Justice Kennedy, Justice Thomas, and Justice Alito would grant the application to vacate the stay of execution.
