FRIDAY, MARCH 6, 2009
CERTIORARI -- SUMMARY DISPOSITION
08-368 AL-MARRI, ALI V. SPAGONE, DANIEL
(08A755)
The application of the Acting Solicitor General respecting
the custody and transfer of petitioner, seeking to release                     petitioner from military custody and transfer him to the custody                of the Attorney General, presented to The Chief Justice and by                him referred to the Court is granted. The judgment is vacated                and the case is remanded to the United States Court of Appeals                for the Fourth Circuit with instructions to dismiss the appeal as           moot. See United States v. Munsingwear, Inc., 340 U.S. 36                     (1950).
