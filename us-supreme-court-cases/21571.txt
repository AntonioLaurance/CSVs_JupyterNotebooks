United States Supreme Court DELAWARE & HUDSON CANAL CO v. COM. OF PENNSYLVANIA, (1895) No. 452 Argued:     Decided: January 14, 1895
J. A. Stranahan and W. U. Hensel, for defendant in error.
Mr. Chief Justice FULLER.
Judgment reversed, with costs, upon the authority of New York, L. E. & W. R. CO. v. Pennsylvania, 153 U.S. 628 , 14 Sup. Ct. 952, and cause remanded, for further proceedings consistent with the opinion in that case.
