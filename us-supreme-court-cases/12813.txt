CERTIORARI -- SUMMARY DISPOSITIONS
05-656 MEDIMMUNE, INC. V. CENTOCOR, INC., ET AL.
The petition for a writ of certiorari is granted. The
judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the United
States Court of Appeals for the Federal Circuit for further
consideration in light of MedImmune, Inc. v. Genentech, Inc.,
549 U. S. ___ (2007). Justice Breyer took no part in the
consideration or decision of this petition.
05-1472 MILLER, SUPT., EASTERN V. RODRIGUEZ, JOSE
The motion of respondent for leave to proceed in forma
pauperis is granted. The petition for a writ of certiorari
is granted. The judgment is vacated and the case is
remanded to the United States Court of Appeals for the Second
Circuit for further consideration in light of Carey v. Musladin,
549 U. S. ___ (2006).
05-1527 SCHMIDT, SHERIFF V. VAN PATTEN, JOSEPH L.
The petition for a writ of certiorari is granted. The
judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the United
States Court of Appeals for the Seventh Circuit for further
consideration in light of Carey v. Musladin, 549 U. S. ___
(2006).
06-7182 MARTINEZ, INES H. V. UNITED STATES
The motion of petitioner for leave to proceed in forma
pauperis and the petition for a writ of certiorari are granted.
The judgment is vacated and the case is remanded to the United
States Court of Appeals for the Eighth Circuit for further
consideration in light of United States v. Booker, 543 U. S. 220
(2005).
