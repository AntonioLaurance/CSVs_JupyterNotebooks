United States Supreme Court HOLLYWOOD BASEBALL ASSOC. v. COMM'R INTERNAL REVENUE, (1966) No. 957 Argued:     Decided: March 28, 1966
Certiorari granted; 352 F.2d 350, vacated and remanded.
Arthur E. Gore for petitioner.
Solicitor General Marshall, Acting Assistant Attorney General Roberts and Harry Baum for respondent.
PER CURIAM.
The petition for a writ of certiorari is granted. The judgment of the Court of Appeals is vacated and the case is remanded to that court for further consideration in light of Malat v. Riddell, ante, p. 569. [383 U.S. 824, 825]
