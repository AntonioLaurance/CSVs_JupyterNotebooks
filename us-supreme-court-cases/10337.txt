United States Supreme Court MORALES v. CITY OF GALVESTON, (1960) No. 167 Argued:     Decided: October 17, 1960
Certiorari granted; judgment vacated; and case remanded.
Reported below: 275 F.2d 191.
Arthur J. Mandell for petitioners.
Preston Shirley for the City of Galveston, and Byron F. Williams for Cardigan Shipping Co., Ltd., respondents.
PER CURIAM.
The motion for leave to proceed in forma pauperis and the petition for writ of certiorari are granted. The judgment of the Court of Appeals is vacated and the case is remanded to that court for consideration in light of Mitchell v. Trawler Racer, Inc., 362 U.S. 539 . [364 U.S. 295, 296]
