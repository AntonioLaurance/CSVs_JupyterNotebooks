United States Supreme Court ROBERTOY v. MICHIGAN, (1960) No. 308 Argued:     Decided: December 19, 1960
Appeal dismissed since the judgment below is based on a nonfederal ground adequate to support it.
Petitioner pro se.
Paul L. Adams, Attorney General of Michigan, and Samuel J. Torina, Solicitor General, for respondent.
PER CURIAM.
The appeal is dismissed for the reason that the judgment of the Supreme Court of Michigan, sought here to be reviewed, is based upon a nonfederal ground adequate to support it. [364 U.S. 519, 520]
