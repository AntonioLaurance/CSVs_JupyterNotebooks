United States Supreme Court FEDERAL SAVINGS AND LOAN v. BOWERS, (1959) No. 537 Argued:     Decided: January 19, 1959
Appeal dismissed and certiorari denied.
Reported below: 168 Ohio St. 65, 151 N. E. 2d 223.
Robert G. Day for appellant.
William Saxbe, Attorney General of Ohio, and John M. Tobin, Assistant Attorney General, for appellee.
PER CURIAM.
The motion to dismiss is granted and the appeal is dismissed. Treating the papers whereon the appeal was taken as a petition for writ of certiorari, certiorari is denied.
MR. JUSTICE HARLAN is of the opinion that probable jurisdiction should be noted. [358 U.S. 305, 306]
