cited therein.

ATTORNEY DISCIPLINE
D-2452 IN THE MATTER OF DISBARMENT OF CHARLES P. MIRARCHI, III

having been suspended from the practice of law in this Court
by order of March 24, 2008; and a rule having been issued and

be disbarred; and a response having been filed;

from the practice of law in this Court.
