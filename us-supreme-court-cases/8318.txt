United States Supreme Court BUSTOP, INC. v. BOARD OF EDUCATION OF CITY OF LOS ANGELES, (1978) Argued:     Decided: September 9, 1978
Application for stay.
Mr. Justice POWELL, Circuit Justice.
The application for a stay in this case denied by Mr. Justice REHNQUIST by his in-chambers opinion and order of September 8, 1978, 439 U.S. 1380 , has now been referred to me.
As I am in accord with the reasons advanced by Mr. Justice REHNQUIST in his opinion, I also deny the application.[ Bustop, Inc. v. Board of Education of City of Los Angeles 439 U.S. 1384 (1978) ]
